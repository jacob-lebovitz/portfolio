How can we then use this information in order to build more sustainable housing options that can be utilized as actual home users instead of absentee owners. As someone who lives in a suburb, I would love to know how flippers decide on houses, and how new builds impact flipping behavior.
    • Source: https://homesforprofit.mapc.org/report

Description of the residential density and housing characteristics of MAPC’s seven housing submarkets and the people who live in them” lists many interesting demographic information about each submarket. I would like to explore how submarket populations can benefit or be harmed through specific investment and/or coroporate behaviors.
